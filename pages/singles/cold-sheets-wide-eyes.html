About This Track

"Cold Sheets, Wide Eyes" captures a moment of raw vulnerability. The imagery of cold sheets represents the emotional distance between two people, while the wide eyes symbolize the intensity of connection and recognition. This track explores the paradox of intimacy—how physical closeness can coexist with emotional distance, and how vulnerability becomes the bridge between two souls.

The production combines ethereal vocals with haunting instrumentals, creating an atmosphere that feels both intimate and haunted. It's a meditation on human connection in its most vulnerable form.
