About This Track

"Two Of Us" is a tender meditation on human connection. The imagery of hands together symbolizes the simple yet profound act of being present with another person. It's about the vulnerability required to truly connect, and the courage it takes to let someone close enough to touch your soul.

The production is warm and intimate, with soft strings and delicate vocals that create a sense of closeness and trust. The lyrics are poetic and introspective, exploring the complexity of relationships and the beauty found in shared moments of vulnerability.
