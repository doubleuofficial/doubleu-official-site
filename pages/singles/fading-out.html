About This Track

"Fading Out" is a cinematic exploration of urban isolation and the beauty found in disappearing. The black and white aesthetic reflects the simplicity of existence—stripped down to its core elements. A figure standing alone in the rain, surrounded by the city's glow, yet completely isolated.

The track captures the moment of letting go, of fading into the background, and finding a strange peace in becoming invisible. It's about the paradox of being surrounded by millions yet feeling utterly alone, and how that solitude can be both painful and liberating.

Musically, "Fading Out" combines introspective lyrics with atmospheric production, creating a soundscape that feels both intimate and expansive—like standing alone in a crowded city at night.
