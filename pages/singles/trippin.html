About This Track

"Trippin'" is a collaborative exploration of consciousness and introspection. The blue neon abstract imagery represents the disorienting yet beautiful experience of losing yourself in thought, of seeing the world from a different perspective. It's about the journey inward and the discoveries made along the way.

The chemistry between DoubleU and Papa Metree creates a dynamic interplay of voices and perspectives. The production is ethereal and hypnotic, with layered synths and intricate beats that mirror the complexity of the human mind. Together, they create a track that feels both deeply personal and universally relatable.
