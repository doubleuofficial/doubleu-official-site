THE VOICE OF THE 405.

Vulnerability in Every Verse

Hailing from the heart of Oklahoma City, **DoubleU** is more than a stage name—it's a reflection of the man behind the music, William. Carving out a unique space in the underground scene, he specializes in a distinctive blend of **melodic soul-trap**, often referred to by fans as "pain music."

The Sound

Moving away from the traditional aggressive tones of trap, DoubleU focuses on conversational, melodic delivery. His music serves as a raw, unfiltered look into personal struggle, growth, and the reality of life in the 405.

Faded 405

"The project isn't just about a city; it's about the shadows we live in and the light we're trying to find."

From the atmospheric rain-soaked vibes of *Found You In The Rain Fall* to the calculated transparency of *Zero Sum*, DoubleU's discography is a testament to the power of melodic storytelling. No belting, no screaming—just the truth, delivered over cinematic production.
