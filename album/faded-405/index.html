About Faded 405

Faded 405 represents DoubleU's artistic vision fully realized. The album title references the artist's roots in Oklahoma City (405 area code), capturing the essence of a place where pain and resilience coexist. Each track is a carefully crafted moment, exploring themes of loss, identity, survival, and hope.

With production that ranges from cinematic and introspective to hard-hitting and visceral, Faded 405 showcases DoubleU's versatility as both a lyricist and artist. The album stands as a testament to authentic storytelling in modern hip-hop, bringing the raw energy of the streets with the sophistication of cinematic production.
