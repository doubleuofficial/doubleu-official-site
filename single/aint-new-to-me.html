About This Track

"Aint New To Me" is a celebration of self-awareness and authenticity. The neon car silhouette represents movement, freedom, and the journey of staying true to yourself. It's about the confidence that comes from knowing exactly who you are, and the refusal to be anything less.

The production is energetic and driving, with pulsing neon-inspired synths that evoke the feeling of cruising through the night with purpose. The lyrics are direct and unapologetic, a statement of intent and identity in a world that constantly tries to redefine us.
